* Thermodynamic Square

** Energy
\[dU = TdS - PdV\]
** Helmholtz free energy (F or A)
\[F = U-TS \]
\[dF = -SdT-PdV\]
** Gibbs free energy
\[G = F+PV = U-TS+PV = \mu N \]
\[dG = -Sdt + VdP\]
** Emphalpy
\[H = U+PV = G+TS\]
\[dH = SdT + VdP\]
